Extraction Process

Wood pressed extraction

The centuries-old traditional oil extraction process is the secret behind the health benefits of our groundnut oil. This method uses a stone as a base and wooden pestle used to gently press the seeds to extract the oil. which helps in extracting all micro-nutrients, taste and aroma from seeds.

We source the best quality seeds directly from the farm to make the groundnut oil. We take utmost care to preserve their true nutrition, flavor and aroma. Seeds dried under the sun for rich taste and aroma.

We use a traditional oil extraction process that rotate at low speed to maintain low temperature during oil extraction. which preserves micro-nutrients and the original flavor and aroma.

We filter our groundnut oil through multiple layers of cotton cloth before packing. To give you a bitter-free taste and splatter-free cooking experience and remove seed sediments.
